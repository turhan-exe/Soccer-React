# Privacy Policy for FHS Manager

**Last updated: February 11, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Nerbuss ("we", "us", or "our") handles your personal information when you use our mobile application FHS Manager (the "App").

## 1. Information Collection
We prioritize your privacy. FHS Manager is designed to collect minimal data.
- **Personal Data:** We do not knowingly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, address, phone number, or email address, unless you voluntarily provide it for support purposes.
- **Usage Data:** The App may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., crash reports, device type) through third-party services like Apple or Google to help us improve the App's stability and performance.

## 2. Third-Party Services
The App may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.
- Google Play Services
- Apple App Store Services

## 3. Data Security
We are committed to ensuring your information is secure. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
